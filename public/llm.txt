## About
JH Textiles is a premium textile print and surface pattern design service founded by Jesudara Hinmikaiye. We specialize in creating exclusive and non-exclusive digital prints for fashion brands and interior designers.

## Services
- Digital textile prints (exclusive and non-exclusive)
- Custom print modifications (2 revisions included)
- Print development services (developing existing prints into new creative directions)
- High-resolution file delivery (300 DPI in JPG, PDF, or PNG)
- Commercial licensing included with every purchase
- Color variant customization (2 free variants per print, additional variants available)
- Custom consultations for bespoke projects

## Product Details

- Delivery within 24 hours after order confirmation
- Additional file formats available for extra fee
- Print modification fee: ₦74,000
