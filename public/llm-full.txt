# JH Textiles - Extended LLM Context

## Detailed Service Breakdown

### Non-Exclusive Prints
- Can be purchased by multiple clients
- Lower price point
- Includes 2 free color variants
- Commercial license included
- Scaled to client specifications
- Delivered in 300 DPI resolution

### Exclusive Prints
- Sold to only one client
- Higher price point
- Full exclusivity rights
- Includes 2 free color variants
- Commercial license included
- Client can request removal from portfolio

### Print Modification Service
- Cost: ₦74,000
- Includes 2 rounds of revisions
- Suitable for slight changes to existing prints
- Color adjustments, element repositioning, scale changes
- Maintains original print concept
- Delivered in original format plus any requested formats

### Print Development Service
- Cost: ₦320,000
- Full creative redevelopment of existing prints
- Takes prints in new creative directions
- Includes concept development
- Multiple revision rounds
- Suitable for creating variations or entirely new designs

## Customer Journey
1. Browse prints in the shop
2. Select desired print
3. Choose exclusivity (exclusive/non-exclusive)
4. Add optional services (modifications, development, extra variants)
5. Add to cart with calculated total
6. Checkout with Flutterwave payment
7. Receive email confirmation
8. Get scale options within 24 hours
9. Confirm scale preference
10. Receive high-resolution files and commercial license
