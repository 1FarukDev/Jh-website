T-Shirt Mask Creator

Instructions:
  1. Upload your image using the file input below
  2. Option A: Click "Auto Remove White" to automatically detect and remove white/light areas
  3. Option B: Manually paint over areas you want to make transparent
  4. Adjust "White Tolerance" to be more/less selective (higher = removes more shades of white)
  5. Use brush for touch-ups if needed
  6. Click "Download Transparent Image" to get your result!

Tip: The auto-removal works great for white t-shirts against dark backgrounds!
